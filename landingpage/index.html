Be Young, Have Fun,
Drink Sprite

In 1956, a team at The Coca-Cola Company developed a tart,
clear-colored carbonated drink and the competition was on. Marketed initially as both a carbonated beverage and a drink mixer,
Sprite was tested in various markets throughout the United States, launching nationally in 1961 to rave reviews.
